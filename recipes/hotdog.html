Pig-Dog

About the Recipe

It's a bird, it's a plane, no it's a Pig-Dog! This mouth-watering bacon wrapped hotdog is out of this world!
The Pig-Dog is a 100% pork dog wrapped in 2 pieces maple bacon. Topped off with grilled onions, tomatoes, mayonnaise,
and cilantro on a glazed bun, I guarantee you this will not be your last time grilling the Pig-Dog!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole 100% pork dog
  • 2 slices of maple bacon
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cilantro
  • Maple Syrup
  • 1 Hotdog Bun

Steps:

  1. Chop desired amount of tomato, cilantro, and onion
  2. Place pork dog on the grill
  3. Pour maple syrup into a bowl and submerge hotdog bun in maple syrup
  4. Remove pork dog from the grill before it is fully cooked and wrap the 2 pieces of bacon around it
  5. Place the pork dog wrapped in bacon back on the grill, along with the onion and hotdog bun
  6. Grill contents to desired finish
  7. Remove contents from the grill
  8. Place pork dog on the bun
  9. Place the tomato, onion, cilantro, and mayonnaise on the bun
  10. Enjoy!
