David's Smoothie

About the Recipe

This beauty is a house special, originally created by legendary chef David Gallardo. Containing 3 different
sources of protein, oats for energy, and a variety of fruits, there is nothing this smoothie is missing.
David's smoothie is a powerhouse drink for any avid gym-rat who is trying to build some serious muscle!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of Whey Protein
  • 1 cup of Almond Milk
  • 1/2 cup of ice
  • 1 whole banana
  • 1 packet of maple brown sugar instant oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Fill blender cup with Almond Milk and ice
  2. Cut the banana in half and place both ends into the blender cup
  3. Insert Whey Protein, oatmeal packet, and peanut butter
  4. Finally, top off the blender cup with the blueberries
  5. Place blender cup on blender and lets it blend for 15 seconds
  6. Pour smoothie into a cup and enjoy the gains!
