Kung-Fu Burger

About the Recipe

I hope you brought your appetite, because you're going to need it to eat this monster.
Containing a 1/2 pound beef patty, pepper jack cheese, bacon, and did I forget to mention
the spices? Each beef patty is marinated in kung pao sauce the night before it will be cooked.
Each burger itself contains sichuan peppercorns, siracha, and Chinese Ghost Pepper that will
be sure to bring the Kung-Fu directly to your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound beef patty
  • 2 Sesame seed buns
  • 2 slice Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Kung Pao Sauce
  • Sichuan Peppercorns
  • Siracha Sauce
  • 1/2 Chinese Ghost Pepper
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Marinate 1/2 pound beef patty in Kung Pao Sauce the night before grilling
  2. Cut Sichuan Peppercorns and Chinese Ghost Peppers
  3. Put beefy patty and bacon on the grill (cook to desired finish)
  4. As the beef patty and bacon near finish, place the Pepper Jack Cheese on the patty
  5. Prepare Sesame Seed buns with Mayonnaise and Siracha sauce
  6. Place finished patty and bacon on the Sesame Seed bun
  7. Top off the burger with the Sichuan Peppercorn and Chinese Ghost Pepper
  8. Buckle-up and enjoy the Kung-Fu Burger!
